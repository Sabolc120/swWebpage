Raids showcase of soulworker, ordered by timeline...

You know an MMORPG is old when there are raids which are too easy

Hidden Hideout

Level 68 raid of the game, and probably almost the most difficult raid in the game, and there are many reasons behind that, mostly bugs.

Difficulty during early stages: ★★★★★

Difficulty during later stages: ★★★

Demand of teamwork: ★★★★

Violent Sun

Level 72 raid of the game, no difficult mechanics, this raid is a damage checking as it has different time limits for each phases.

Difficulty during early stages: ★★★★

Difficulty during later stages: ★★

Demand of teamwork: ★★★

Broken Savior

Level 76 raid of the game, it has a normal and hard mode. The hard mode is very difficult due so much little damages, you need to be very careful to keep yourself alive.

Difficulty during normal stages: ★★★

Difficulty during hard stages: ★★★★★

Demand of teamwork: ★★★★★
